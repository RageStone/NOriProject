Family Guy

This website provides information about the series in a simple and cool way, while also making it aesthetic and pleasing to explore.

Family Guy is an animation sitcom series created by Seth MacFarlane in 1999 and is still running till this day.
The Show centers around the Griffin family: Peter and Lois, Their children, Meg, Chris and Stewie, and their talking dog, Brian.
